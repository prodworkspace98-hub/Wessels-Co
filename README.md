 
The Wessels & Co Accounting Portfolio is a professional business website designed to showcase accounting services, company information, and client engagement options. The project focuses on creating a clean, trustworthy, and user-friendly interface that reflects the credibility and professionalism expected in the financial services industry.

The website typically includes sections such as an overview of services (e.g., tax consulting, auditing, bookkeeping), an about page, contact information, and calls-to-action encouraging potential clients to get in touch. The design emphasizes clarity, accessibility, and ease of navigation to ensure a seamless user experience.
